The problem

Everything feels temporarily important — so nothing ever gets resolved.

We screenshot. We save. We tell ourselves we'll sort it out later. We never do. The result: thousands of files you can't delete — but also can't find when you actually need them.

I'll organize this later.
(never happens)

I know I saved something like this… it's faster to just Google it again.

I can't go through 5,000 photos now. What if this one is actually important?

I'll delete the bad ones later. Don't close it — I still need it.

The solution

Two ways to tackle your digital clutter.

Whether you're cleaning up your own device or the apps you use every day — we've got you covered. No forced deletion. No anxiety. You stay in control at every step.

Part 1

For your device

A smart companion that lives on your phone and helps you evaluate what's actually worth keeping.

  • 📸
    Photos — swipe to archive
    Swipe left to archive a photo — it doesn't delete right away. It stays hidden for a set period. If you don't restore it or use it, it's gone. No regret, no pressure.
  • 📄
    Documents — preview before deciding
    Same swipe system for files. Tap to preview the document first, then decide — keep or archive. Works for PDFs, notes, downloads.
  • 🔍
    Background checks
    Downloaded something with the same name as an existing file? Duplicate photo detected? A pop-up asks if you want to keep both — no silent overwriting.
  • 📱
    Unused apps — with real stats
    See which apps you haven't opened in 30, 60 or 90 days — with usage data. Decide what's actually earning its place on your screen.
Part 2

For the apps you already use

An embedded layer that plugs into existing platforms — so you can clean up where the clutter actually lives.

  • ▶️
    YouTube Watch Later — finally useful
    Our API embeds directly into YouTube's Watch Later. From time to time, a card pops up: "Still interested in watching this?" Swipe to keep or let it go.
  • 🔗
    Embedded in your main feed
    No need to open a separate app. The nudge appears naturally inside the platform you're already using — non-intrusive, on your schedule.
  • 🤖
    AI-powered photo categories
    Our AI automatically sorts your photos by category — receipts, screenshots, memories, work — so you can review by topic, not by endless scroll.
